01 Workflow Documentation — ▶

Are your AI processes mapped, written down, and understood across the team?

Undocumented workflows make AI systems fragile. One person leaving can collapse an entire AI-powered process.

02 Prompt Governance — ▶

Are your prompts versioned, owned, tested, and controlled?

Uncontrolled prompts are the #1 source of inconsistent AI output. Most companies treat prompts like throwaway text — until something goes wrong publicly.

03 Human Oversight — ▶

Are there defined review processes, escalation paths, and human checkpoints in your AI workflows?

EU AI Act requirements, customer trust, and operational safety all depend on human oversight being systematic — not occasional.

04 Output Monitoring — ▶

Is AI output quality tracked, measured, and evaluated over time?

AI systems degrade silently. Without monitoring, you won't know your model is producing bad outputs until the damage is done.

05 Data & Knowledge Quality — ▶

Is the information your AI relies on accurate, structured, and maintained?

Garbage in, garbage out. The quality of your AI's knowledge base directly determines the quality of every output it produces.

06 Compliance Readiness — ▶

Is your team aware of AI regulation obligations and actively preparing for them?

The EU AI Act is in force. Non-compliance for high-risk AI systems carries significant penalties. Governance built now costs a fraction of remediation later.
